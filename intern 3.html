Key-Board

A musical keyboard is an electronic or acoustic instrument with a set of keys that produce musical sounds when pressed. It typically has 61, 76, or 88 keys arranged like a piano and can generate various instrument tones, rhythms, and effects. Digital keyboards use synthesized or sampled sounds, often featuring MIDI compatibility, touch sensitivity, and built-in accompaniments. They come in different types, including digital pianos, synthesizers, workstations, and MIDI controllers, catering to beginners and professionals. Lightweight and portable, keyboards are widely used in various music genres for performance, composition, and recording.
